Archery Club

Information introduction

Welcome everyone to come to the archery club and please contact the teachers in charge of the club before the start of each term, so that teachers will be able to know how many people will participate.

When and where is the club taking place

The archery club is taking place at the Lower Fields beside the PE Office. The playing days are: Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday & Friday lunchtimes; and Friday after-school.
